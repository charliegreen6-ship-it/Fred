# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Fred is a private personal assistant application developed solely for the personal use of Charles Green.

## What data is collected

Fred may access authorised third-party services, such as Oura, in order to provide planning, health insights and organisational assistance to the account owner.

The information accessed may include health and activity information, including sleep, readiness and activity metrics.

## How the data is used

The data is used only to provide personal assistance, planning support and health-related insights to the account owner.

Fred does not sell, rent or share personal information with third parties.

## Data storage

Data processed by Fred is stored only on infrastructure controlled by the account owner.

Authorisation tokens required to access third-party services are stored securely and used solely to provide the requested functionality.

## Contact

This application is not intended for public use.

Questions regarding this application may be directed to the account owner.
